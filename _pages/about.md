Dr. Ao is currently an Assistant Research Fellow at the School of Automation, Chongqing University. He received his Ph.D. degree from Chongqing University in 2025 and his B.Eng. degree from Southeast University in 2019.

His research interests focus on stability analysis, resilience assessment, and advanced control technologies for both aeronautical hybrid power supply systems and DC microgrids. He has published numerous peer-reviewed research papers in leading journals including IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics (IEEE TIE), IEEE Journal of Emerging and Selected Topics in Power Electronics (IEEE JESTPE), and Transactions of China Electrotechnical Society. Dr. Ao is a member of the IEEE P3560 Standards Committee. He serves as a regular reviewer for multiple core journals such as IEEE TIE, and has held the position of Session Chair at academic conferences including the IEEE Conference on Industrial Electronics and Applications (IEEE ICIEA).
